Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Yumvote is a household meal planning app operated by Blinklabs Ltd ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and the choices you have. The short version: we store what's needed to run a shared household experience, payments never touch our servers, and we don't sell personal data.

What we collect

  • Account details — your name, email address, and avatar, provided through Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Food preferences — dietary styles, allergens, and disliked ingredients you choose to set.
  • Household activity — meal plans, votes, dish ratings, recipes you create, and shopping lists, shared with the members of your household.
  • Device tokens — a push notification token so your household's updates can reach your phone.

What we don't collect

  • Payment details. Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We only receive confirmation that a subscription is active, never card numbers.
  • Location. The app does not request or track your location.
  • Advertising identifiers. Yumvote contains no third-party advertising and does not track you across other apps or websites.

How your data is used

Only to make the app work: syncing plans, votes, and shopping lists between household members in real time, warning your household about allergens and preferences when planning meals, sending the notifications you'd expect (votes opening, plans locking, shopping updates), and understanding aggregate app usage so we can improve it.

Who can see what

Members of your household see your name, avatar, preferences, votes, ratings, and shopping activity, because that's the point of a shared household. Nothing about you is visible to other households or the public.

Services we rely on

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status management (receives your user ID and purchase state from the store, never payment details).
  • Expo — push notification delivery and anonymous usage counts.
  • Apple and Google — sign-in and payment processing under their own privacy policies.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. You can delete your account at any time inside the app: open the Me tab and choose "Delete my account". Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, memberships, ratings, and push tokens. If you are the only admin of a household, that household and its data are deleted with you. Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately in your App Store or Play Store settings.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the EU GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or erase your personal data. Most of this you can do directly in the app; for anything else, email us and we'll help.

Children

Yumvote accounts are intended for ages 13 and up. Younger children can exist in a household as dependent profiles created and managed by a parent or guardian, without their own account or login.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes, we'll update this page and note it in the app. The date at the top always reflects the latest revision.

Contact

Anything else: support@yumvote.app
